Microphone

Google Meet does not play your own voice back into the tab, so the recorder also captures your microphone to include what you say. Grant access once; it is mixed with the meeting audio while recording.

Recording consent

You are responsible for informing all meeting participants that the call is being recorded, in accordance with local laws and your organization’s policies.
